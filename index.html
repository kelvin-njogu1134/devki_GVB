In an Emergency?

If you are in danger or need to report a Gender Based Violence incident, click the emergency button below. Your location and the current time will be recorded and sent to our call center for immediate assistance.

DEMO MODE: Locations shown will be around Ruiru area

How it works:

  • Click the EMERGENCY ALERT button when you need help
  • For this demo, fake locations around Ruiru will be generated
  • Your alert will be saved and sent to the call center dashboard
  • Call center operators will see your location on a map
  • All data is stored locally in your browser for this demonstration
